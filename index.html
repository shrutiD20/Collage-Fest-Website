Ashayein

The Cultural Fest

The college cultural fest is a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity,
and diversity. It brings together students, faculty, and staff to showcase
their artistic and cultural prowess through various events such as music
competitions, theater plays, art exhibitions, and poetry recitations.
With colorful decorations, lively music, and enthusiastic participation, the
fest fosters a sense of community spirit and provides a platform for students
to express themselves, learn from each other, and forge lifelong memories.
It's a time of joy, camaraderie, and celebration of the rich cultural tapestry
within the college campus.

Esperanza

The Tech Fest

The college technical fest is an exhilarating display of innovation,
intellect, and technological prowess. It serves as a platform for students
to exhibit their engineering skills, scientific knowledge, and problem-solving
abilities through a variety of competitions, workshops, and exhibitions.
From robotics challenges to coding hackathons, from scientific research
presentations to engineering design contests, the fest is a hub of
cutting-edge technology and forward-thinking ideas. It fosters collaboration,
encourages experimentation, and inspires the next generation of innovators
and entrepreneurs. The technical fest is a testament to the college's commitment
to academic excellence and technological advancement.
